1. Project Overview & Purpose

Quazenthor (QWBQ) is a standard, fixed-supply BEP-20 token deployed on BNB Smart Chain mainnet as a public educational reference implementation.

2. Smart Contract Structure & Immutability

The contract is a clean, minimal implementation of the BEP-20 standard with no additional logic, no administrative functions, and no modifiable parameters after deployment.

Implemented functions: transfer, transferFrom, approve, allowance, balanceOf, totalSupply, name, symbol, decimals.

  • No minting function ‚Äî supply cannot increase
  • No burning mechanism ‚Äî supply cannot decrease
  • No transfer fees or taxation
  • Ownership retained (no privileged functions)
  • No upgradeability or proxy pattern

The contract is immutable and serves as a stable reference for studying standard BEP-20 behavior on a live network.
