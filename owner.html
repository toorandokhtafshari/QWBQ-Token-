Contract Ownership

The Quazenthor (QWBQ) smart contract ownership is retained by the deployer address to preserve the stability and immutability of the educational reference implementation.

View verified signature → #15181

Purpose of Retaining Ownership

Retaining ownership (with no privileged functions) ensures:

  • The reference implementation remains unchanged and consistent
  • The educational model stays permanently fixed as originally deployed

The contract contains no owner-only functions such as minting, burning, pausing, upgrading, fee changes, or any form of administrative control.

Contract Immutability

  • No minting capability exists
  • No burning mechanism exists
  • No taxation or fee structure exists
  • No upgrade proxy or implementation pointer exists
  • No pause, blacklist, or freeze functions exist

After deployment, the contract logic is permanently fixed.
