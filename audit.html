3. Key Findings & Security Assessment

No critical or medium issues were identified. The contract is considered low-risk due to its minimal and standard design:

  • Fixed Supply: Total supply is permanently fixed at 5,000,000,000,000 (5.00T) QWBQ with 18 decimals. No mint or burn functions can be called after deployment.
  • No Fees or Taxes: All transfers are direct with no deductions or custom logic.
  • No Administrative Controls: No owner privileges exist to pause transfers, modify balances, change metadata, or perform any sensitive actions post-deployment.
  • Immutable Code: The contract cannot be upgraded or modified.
  • Standard Functions Only: Implements only the following BEP-20 functions: totalSupply, balanceOf, transfer, transferFrom, approve, allowance, name, symbol, decimals.

Overall Risk Level: Low. The absence of custom logic and privileged roles eliminates most common attack vectors. All claims are directly verifiable via the public source code on BscScan.
