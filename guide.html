Purpose of This Guide

This page helps visitors understand the structure of the QUAZENTHOR website and quickly find information about the project, including:

  • Project purpose and educational role
  • Smart contract details and verification
  • Transparency documentation
  • Deployer and contributor information
  • Official resources and contact channels

The website serves as the central source of documentation for the QUAZENTHOR educational reference token — a fixed-supply BEP-20 contract deployed on BNB Smart Chain mainnet for real-world interaction and study.

Main Sections Overview

  • Core — Home page, About, general project information
  • Token — Tokenomics, current holders, lifecycle & on-chain behavior
  • Team & Identity — Contributors, deployer information, founder declaration, network links
  • Verification — Ownership verification, contract verification, BscScan details, official token information
  • Compliance — Explorer compliance notes, domain & email confirmation, communication channels
  • Community — News & updates, blog (educational content), community guidelines, contact, FAQ
  • Security — Security considerations, contract audit/verification notes
  • Documents — Whitepaper, footer information, changelog
  • Scope — Scope declaration
  • Site Guide — This page (how to navigate the site)
  • Legal — Legal notice, privacy policy, terms, license

Quick Navigation Tips

Use the dropdown menu at the top of every page to jump between categories.

All important verification links (BscScan, ownership signature, contract address) are available on multiple pages for easy access.
