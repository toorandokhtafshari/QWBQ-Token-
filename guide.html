Purpose of This Site Guide

This guide helps users, reviewers (including blockchain explorers such as BscScan), and interested parties understand the complete structure of the QUAZENTHOR website and easily locate all transparency, verification, identity disclosure, and educational content.

The entire website exists exclusively for documentation, public accountability, team identity disclosure, smart contract transparency, and educational reference. There are no financial features, promotional content, investment-related materials, token sales, or utility claims anywhere on the site.

QUAZENTHOR (QWBQ) is a fixed-supply, immutable BEP-20 token deployed solely as a permanent public on-chain reference example. It allows learners, developers, and researchers to observe and study standard BEP-20 contract behavior in a real, live network environment without any administrative controls, upgrades, or economic mechanisms.

The project is complete at deployment — there is no roadmap, no future development plans, no milestones, no updates to the smart contract, and no utility beyond serving as an educational demonstration.

All pages are designed to provide maximum clarity and verifiability. This documentation is published specifically for transparency and review purposes.
