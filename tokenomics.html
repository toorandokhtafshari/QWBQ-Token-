3. Supply Logic & On-Chain Enforcement

The total supply was minted in a single transaction at deployment to the deployer address. This is verifiable on BscScan via the contract creation transaction.

No minting functions: The contract does not include any mint, create, or increaseSupply functions — supply is capped forever.

No burning functions: No burn, destroy, or decreaseSupply functions exist — supply cannot be reduced.

Deployer Wallet Clarification: The deployer wallet holds the initial supply solely for technical reasons (standard BEP-20 deployment). It is not a treasury, liquidity pool, or distribution fund. No sales, distributions, or transfers have occurred or are planned.

Token transfers do not represent fundraising, sales, or capital raising activities.

4. Transfer Mechanics & Neutral Economics

Transfers follow standard BEP-20 logic: sender balance decreases, receiver balance increases by exact amount specified.

No fees or taxes: 100% of the transferred amount reaches the receiver — no deductions, reflections, or redistributions.

No blacklists or restrictions: Any valid address can send/receive tokens without approval or limitations.

No pause or freeze: The contract cannot be paused; transfers always possible as long as the network is operational.

No owner privileges: No renounceOwnership or transferOwnership functions — deployment address has no special controls.
