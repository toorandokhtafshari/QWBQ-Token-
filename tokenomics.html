Supply Structure Clarification

Total supply is permanently fixed.

No minting exists.
No burning exists.
No taxation exists.
No upgradeability exists.

This ensures permanent stability of the reference contract.

Educational distribution is being carried out gradually and all changes are transparently visible on-chain.

There is no possibility of minting or increasing the supply, and the total supply remains fixed forever.

This structure maintains the stability of the reference contract while allowing real on-chain educational interaction.

There is no liquidity pool, as the token is designed for educational interaction rather than trading.

Core Token Parameters (Permanently Immutable & Fully On-Chain Defined)

These parameters are hard-coded in the contract bytecode at deployment and cannot be changed:

  • Name: quazenthor – Permanently fixed string, no modification functions exist.
  • Symbol: QWBQ – Permanently fixed string, no modification functions exist.
  • Decimals: 18 – Standard BEP-20 precision, immutable.
  • Total Supply: 5,000,000,000,000 QWBQ (5.00T) – Exactly this amount, minted at deployment and permanently fixed. No minting, burning, or supply adjustment functions exist in the contract.
  • Contract Address: 0x013f5f2F7F5b027012415A783ac2ed69EF936aE8 – Deployed on BNB Smart Chain and fully verified on BscScan (exact bytecode match).
  • Standard: BEP-20 – Ensures compatibility with BNB Smart Chain wallets and tools.

Supply Logic & On-Chain Enforcement

The total supply was minted in a single transaction at deployment to the deployer address. This is verifiable on BscScan via the contract creation transaction.

The contract does not include any mint, create, or increaseSupply functions — supply is capped forever.

No burn, destroy, or decreaseSupply functions exist — supply cannot be reduced.

The deployer wallet holds the initial supply for technical deployment reasons.

Transfer Mechanics

Transfers follow standard BEP-20 logic: sender balance decreases, receiver balance increases by exact amount specified.

100% of the transferred amount reaches the receiver — no deductions, reflections, or redistributions.

Any valid address can send/receive tokens without approval or limitations.

The contract cannot be paused; transfers always possible as long as the network is operational.

No Fees, Taxes, or Economic Mechanisms

Quazenthor has zero built-in fees of any kind:

  • No transaction taxes
  • No liquidity fees
  • No marketing/development wallets
  • No token reflections or holder rewards
  • No anti-whale limits or transfer caps

The only cost is standard BNB network gas fees, unrelated to the token contract.
