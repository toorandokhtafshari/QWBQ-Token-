The smart contract for QUAZENTHOR is designed to be as simple and secure as possible. This minimal design reduces risks and makes it easy to understand for educational purposes.

  • No Mint Function: There is no way to create new tokens after deployment. This ensures the total supply remains fixed forever, preventing inflation or unexpected changes. Why? To demonstrate supply immutability in a real token.
  • No Burn Function: Tokens cannot be destroyed or removed from circulation by anyone, including the owner. This keeps the supply predictable. Why? To show a truly fixed-supply model without reduction mechanisms.
  • No Fees or Taxes on Transactions: Every transfer is standard and free of any automatic deductions, rewards, or redistributions. Why? To avoid complexity and focus on basic token behavior.
  • No Blacklist, Pause, or Freeze Functions: No ability to block addresses or stop the contract. All users can interact freely. Why? To emphasize decentralization and lack of central control.
  • No Upgradeable Features: The contract cannot be changed or upgraded after deployment. It's permanent as-is. Why? To highlight immutability, a key blockchain principle.
  • No Owner Control Over Balances: The owner cannot access, move, or alter anyone's tokens. Why? To prevent any risk of misuse and build trust through code.
